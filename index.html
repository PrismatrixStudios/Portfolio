About Me

Hello! I'm a passionate web developer with 4 years of experience, primarily focusing on creating websites that provide mental health assistance and support. My mission is to make mental health resources more accessible through technology.

Throughout my journey, I've dedicated myself to developing platforms that offer comfort, guidance, and support to those seeking mental health assistance. One of my notable projects is a comprehensive mental health resource website, which you can explore at Mind & Wellness.

I believe in creating user-centric designs that not only look beautiful but also serve a meaningful purpose. My expertise includes responsive web design, user interface development, and creating intuitive user experiences that make mental health resources easily accessible to everyone.
